Aquais


‹ ›

Aquais is an all-around diffucult map deisgned for Minecraft Experts in the Art of Parkour. It tests a Minecrafter's perserverance while providing a fun,diffucult enivorment for the player. Although most will not suceed the few who do should be considered the TRUE experts of Minecraft. Do you have what it takes to be the TRUE EXPERT of Minecraft?

Aquais is a map made on the Project Conquest Server Build Section. The Survival Section of this server will be made public very soon. World Edit was used to make the large walls and the Orginal one layer Bedrock Map was generated in Single Player.

This is not a completed map and it may never be. Me and my other builders plan to increase the size of the map and include the new features of many updates to come. As a result, the map is considered only 5% complete. However, it is still very much playable. I plan to add more abstract obstacles. Commenting your feedback is beneficial.

Please do not rage in the coments and claiming that some parts are impossible. I have personally tested each and every obsticle in Adventure mode. You can either private message me or post a comment if you have any new Ideas.

Good Luck Minecrafters and may you discover the secrets of Aquais
